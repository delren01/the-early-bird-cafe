~ About Us ~

The Early Bird Cafe is your cozy neighborhood spot for refreshing and delicious drinks. We serve a wide selection of classic and specialty milk teas, flavored lattes, iced teas, fruit teas, fruity yogurts, and more. With warm wood furnishings, inviting lighting, and a peaceful atmosphere, it’s the perfect place to relax, catch up with friends, or enjoy a quiet moment with your favorite drink.
